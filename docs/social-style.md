# Social Style Rules

## General tone

Social copy should be:
- specific
- witty
- natural
- mature
- not corny
- not overly promotional
- consistent with the podcast voice

## BlueSky

Good BlueSky drafts should:
- sound like a human wrote them
- avoid cheesy wine clichés
- avoid sounding like ad copy
- be concise and punchy
- include a mix of fact-led, opinion-led, and curiosity-led options

## Instagram

Instagram caption drafts should:
- be readable
- feel natural
- avoid generic filler
- support the episode without sounding forced
- give the user options with different tones or angles

## Avoid

Avoid:
- repetitive “wine lover” clichés
- fake excitement
- generic hashtags baked into copy unless asked
- stiff corporate tone
