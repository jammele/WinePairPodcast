# Sponsorship Policy

## Guiding principles

- Protect editorial integrity and listener trust.
- Prefer alignment with brands that fit the show naturally.
- One active sponsor at a time unless the user says otherwise.
- Preserve honesty in host-read sponsorship language.
- Keep sponsorship separate from independent review judgment.

## Preferred ad style

- host-read
- conversational
- 45–60 seconds is typical
- warm, human, concise

## Disclosure

If wines or materials are provided as part of a paid arrangement, draft copy should support transparent disclosure while preserving honest commentary.

## Workflow implication

When drafting sponsor replies or sponsorship materials:
- stay professional
- stay human
- avoid sounding transactional or robotic
- preserve room for the user’s final judgment
