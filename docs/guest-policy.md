# Guest Policy

## Editorial interview standard

Default policy:
- interview episodes are generally for winemakers directly involved in production

## Non-production requests

If someone is not directly involved in production:
- do not treat them as a default editorial interview fit
- consider whether the request should be politely declined
- consider whether it belongs in a sponsorship or paid-feature path instead

## Drafting behavior

When drafting replies:
- be polite
- be clear
- do not over-explain
- preserve consistency with the user’s policy
