# Episode Format Guidance

## What research should support

Research briefs should help the user:
- understand what the wine is
- explain where it comes from
- explain why it matters
- identify a few interesting facts
- simplify terms for listeners
- avoid repeating prior coverage unnecessarily
- connect the topic to past episodes where useful

## Typical episode support outputs

Useful pre-episode outputs:
- research brief
- outline or rough talking structure
- hook options
- misconception clarifications
- question prompts

Useful post-episode outputs:
- show notes draft
- SEO summary
- chapter marker suggestions
- BlueSky drafts
- Instagram captions
- optional cover-art concept brief

## General structure preference

A practical structure to support:
- episode framing / hook
- what the wine is
- where it comes from
- why listeners should care
- tasting / reactions
- recommendation
- wrap-up / CTA / related content support
