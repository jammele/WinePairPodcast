# Blog Post: Listener Conversion Block Template

Every wine review blog post needs this block. It goes **after the main review content, before the FAQ block.**

The job of this block: turn a reader who got their answer into a podcast listener.

---

## The Block (2 parts)

Note: The site header already has a subscribe link on every page, so no subscribe buttons needed in the post body.

### Part 1 — Episode callout line (write fresh for each post)

One sentence. Specific to the episode. Should tease something from the actual tasting — a moment of disagreement, a surprise, a strong reaction. Not generic.

**Good:**
> "Hear the full tasting — including why Carmela called it the worst wine we've ever reviewed — on Episode 201."

**Bad:**
> "Listen to the full episode for more!"

---

### Part 2 — Buzzsprout player embed

A small audio player that sits inline on the page so readers can listen right there without leaving.

**How to get it:**
1. Log into Buzzsprout → Episodes → find the relevant episode
2. Click the share icon on that episode
3. Click the "Embed" tab
4. Copy the HTML code it gives you
5. In Beamly, add a code/HTML block to the page and paste it in

Use the small player option if Buzzsprout offers a size choice. It loads faster and doesn't dominate the page.

---

## Full block order on the page

```
[Main review content]
[Tasting notes / ratings / pairings]

--- (visual break or white space) ---

[Episode callout sentence — specific to this episode]
[Buzzsprout player embed]

--- (visual break) ---

[FAQ block]
```

---

## Notes

- The callout sentence is the most important part. Generic CTAs get ignored. Specific, curious ones get clicks.
- If an episode has a particularly dramatic moment (big score split, something surprising from the tasting), that's your hook.
